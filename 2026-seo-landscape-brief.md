# 2026 SEO Landscape Brief

The 2026 SEO landscape is shifting from traditional rankings alone to a broader visibility battle across Google results, AI Overviews, map packs, review ecosystems, and answer engines. Winning brands will need technically sound sites, strong local authority, fresh service-area content, credible reviews, and structured evidence that makes it easy for search and AI systems to understand who they help and why they are trustworthy. For law firms and other high-intent local businesses, the biggest opportunity is no longer just “ranking for keywords,” but owning the full decision path: showing up in local discovery, answering urgent buyer questions clearly, proving expertise with useful content, and converting that visibility into booked consultations before competitors capture the demand.
