Recommended action plan

1. Move Birmingham terms into the top 10

Strengthen the homepage and core criminal defense page around Birmingham-specific proof, internal links, FAQs, and consultation intent.

2. Rebuild underperforming practice pages

Prioritize traffic violations, misdemeanors, identity theft, suspended license, and post-conviction relief pages with clearer client questions and local examples.

3. Reduce homepage weight

Compress heavy media, defer noncritical scripts, and tighten third-party tags to improve first impression speed without changing the brand feel.

4. Capture AI-search answers

Add direct Q&A blocks for criminal defense scenarios so answer engines can cite Revill for common Birmingham legal questions.

5. Build local authority clusters

Create supporting articles for Alabama-specific criminal defense topics that link back into money pages and consultation paths.

6. Track consultation-ready demand

Measure rankings and calls by practice area so growth is tied to qualified cases, not generic traffic.
