## Executive take

Aldous Law already has meaningful organic strength in Dallas personal injury search, but the rankings show a clear near-term opportunity: protect and expand top-page Dallas personal injury visibility while building a stronger medical malpractice growth lane.

The firm is close enough to the money terms to justify a focused SEO proposal. Several high-value Dallas injury terms are already ranking on page 1 or page 2, while medical malpractice terms show very high search demand but weak rankings in the 60s to 90s.

## Primary opportunity

### 1. Move strong Dallas personal injury rankings from “visible” to “dominant”

Aldous Law is already showing up for several Dallas personal injury terms, but many are sitting just outside the most valuable organic positions or near the lower part of page 1.

Priority terms:

- dallas personal injury lawyer: rank 14, 8,100 monthly searches, $163.59 CPC
- injury attorney dallas tx: rank 18, 8,100 monthly searches, $115.67 CPC
- dallas tx personal injury attorneys: rank 14, 8,100 monthly searches, $163.59 CPC

This is the fastest proposal angle: the firm is not starting from zero. A focused content, internal linking, authority, and local pack strategy could turn existing visibility into more qualified case inquiries.

## Likely pitch angle

Aldous Law appears to have strong brand authority and some high-value Dallas personal injury traction. The opportunity is not a generic “you need SEO” pitch. It is more specific:

> Aldous Law is already earning visibility in Dallas personal injury search. The opportunity is to turn those page 1 and page 2 rankings into a stronger case pipeline, then apply the same discipline to medical malpractice terms where the firm has the page, the authority, and the market demand, but not the rankings yet.

## Recommended next moves

1. **Dallas personal injury quick wins**
   - Improve the homepage and personal injury page around the strongest Dallas injury terms.
   - Add clearer internal links from case results, attorney bios, and practice pages back into the Dallas personal injury hub.
   - Strengthen local relevance signals for Dallas and nearby high-value suburbs.

2. **Medical malpractice rebuild**
   - Rework the medical malpractice page into a stronger case-intent landing page.
   - Add supporting pages for hospital negligence, surgical errors, birth injury overlap, delayed diagnosis, and emergency room errors.
   - Build internal links from relevant verdicts, case results, and attorney proof points.

3. **Local pack and AI-search readiness**
   - Map which priority terms trigger local pack and AI Overview results.
   - Strengthen firm profile consistency, review language, attorney/entity signals, and FAQ-style answer coverage.
   - Make the firm easier for Google and AI answer engines to associate with Dallas personal injury and medical malpractice cases.
